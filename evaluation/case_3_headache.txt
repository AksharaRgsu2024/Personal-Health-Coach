**LIFESTYLE RECOMMENDATIONS**
1. **Posture & ergonomics** – Ensure proper sitting/standing posture; use supportive chairs and take short breaks every 30 min.
2. **Gentle stretching & mobility** – Light stretches for the neck, shoulders, and back can relieve muscle tension.
3. **Heat/ice therapy** – Apply a warm compress or cold pack (15–20 min) to the painful area, whichever feels more soothing.
4. **Hydration & nutrition** – Adequate water intake and a balanced diet help muscle function and reduce inflammation.
5. **Stress management** – Practice deep‑breathing, mindfulness, or gentle yoga to reduce muscle tension and pain perception.
6. **Sleep hygiene** – Use a supportive mattress and pillow; avoid sleeping on the affected side if it worsens pain.
